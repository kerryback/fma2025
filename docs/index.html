About

This presentation explores how educators can effectively incorporate AI tools into their teaching methods and curriculum. Topics include:

  • Historical context of technology in education
  • Current AI capabilities and tools
  • Practical applications for classroom use
  • Teaching students about AI
  • Assessment strategies in the AI era
